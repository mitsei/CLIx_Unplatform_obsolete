Role Play

Read the question options, click the arrow keys to choose one.
Discuss with your partner and write a dialogue.
After you have practised record it on the computer and click ‘Submit’.
You can listen to some examples before you begin.
Some words are given in the word cloud on the left of your screen.
You can use some of these words if needed.
After you record and submit, do NOT close the browser.
Move to the group next to you. When they finish their recording, fill the Peer Assessment Rubric to give your friends your feedback.
